Drill Commands

print Print

Unfortunately no training manuals from the 1st century have survived. The closest surviving reference comes from the Strategikon which was written by Flavius Mauricius Tiberius Augustus who ruled Byzantium from 582-602 AD.

With little historical information, Legio XXX along with other local re-enactment groups, established the following drill manual based on what we do know & modern military drill.

Latin English Description
General
Ad arma Fall In Fall into position to the left of the vexillum (standard); stand at ease.
Mandata captate Attention Stand at attention; legs together, arms at side, equipment off the ground.
Laxate At Ease Relax with feet shoulder's width apart, equipment may rest on the ground; maintain discipline (don't move feet, face the front & remain quite).
Vos Dimitto Dismissed Fall out of formation
Dressing Ranks
Frontem dirigite Right Dress: Muster Spacing (1.5') Signifer stands fast, everyone else raises their right elbow, placing their hand on their hip and shifting so their elbow touches the person to their right.
Spatium Date Right Dress: Close Order (3') Signifer stands fast, everyone else raises their right arm parallel to the ground with a closed fist & shifts so their fist touches the person to their right on the shoulder.
Extendite Right Dress: Open Order (6') Everyone extends both arms parallel to the ground with closed fists & shifts so everyone is standing fist to fist with the people on their left & right.
Intrăte Form Two Ranks Starting from the right, omitting the signifer, sound-off alternating unus and duo. At the nunc command, soldiers numbered duo take one step back, and then to the right so they are directly behind the man to their right.
Iungite Close Ranks Tightens up the spacing in the ranks created by Intrăte. Similar to Spatium Date.
Exite Form Single Rank Following Spatium Date, the rear rank takes one step to the left and then forward creating a single rank.
Turning
Ad gladios clinate Right Face Turn 90° to the right, pivoting on the right heal & left toes.
Ad scuta clinate Left Face Turn 90° to the left, pivoting on the left heal & right toes.
Transformate About Face Turn 180° to the right, pivoting on the right heal & left toes.
Ad scutum transformate About Face to the Left Turn 180° to the left, pivoting on the left heal & right toes.
Marching
Tarde movete Slow march Step off with the left foot, maintain a pace of 60 steps/minute.
Movete March Step off with the left foot, maintain a pace of 120 steps/minute.
Duplex tempus Double time Step off with the left foot, maintain a pace of 180 steps/minute.
State Halt Issued on the right step, stop on the left foot and stand at attention.
Ad gladios rotate Right Wheel The first rank swings 90° to the right like a door. People on the outside must take larger steps, while people on the inside take smaller steps. Following ranks follow the person in-front of them.
Ad scuta rotate Left Wheel The first rank swings 90° to the left like a door. People on the outside must take larger steps, while people on the inside take smaller steps. Following ranks follow the person in-front of them.
Formations
Agmen formate Marching column Step off with the left foot, maintain a pace of 120 steps/minute.
Aciem formate Battle line Soldiers form a line with their pilum at the ready. This is not a shield wall, 6' spacing should be maintained between each column.
Cuneum formate Wedge Centerio is placed at the tip of the wedge with soldiers trailing off his side.
Orbem formate Circle Form a defensive circle with ranged units & officers in the center.
Quadratum formate Square Form a defensive square with ranged units & officers in the center.
Testudinem formate Testudo (Tortis) First rank raises shields to eye height, the following ranks lift their shields onto their heads overlapping the rank in-front. Formation advances together using a chant to stay in step ("Ro-ma, Ro-ma" or "Dex-Sin, Dex-Sin").
Porro Advance Move forward keeping formation with weapons drawn.
Ordinem servate Hold position Stop moving, maintain formation order.
Weapons: Pilum
Pila deponite Lay down javelin Lay pilum flat on the ground.
Pila infigite Plant javelin Stick pilum vertically into the ground.
Pila tollite Pickup javelin Retrieve pilum from ground and hold in right hand.
Pila parate Prepare to throw Second rank should a step to the left to offset them selves from first rank. Place left foot ahead & raise your pilum to shoulder level.
Pila iacite Throw First rank advance 3 steps and throws their pilum. After, the 2nd rank does the same through the gaps in the 1st rank. Immediately draw your sword after throwing.
Weapons: Gladius
Gladios stringite Draw swords Unsheathe sword from scabbard using right hand.
Gladios recondite Sheath swords Place sword back into scabbard using right hand.
Weapons: Artillery
Funditor Slingers Following command is for slingers only.
Sagittarii Archers Following command is for archers only.
Ballistam Bolt thrower Following command is for scropios / balistas.
Catapultam Catapult Following command is for catapults only.
[Type] torquete Wind / cock weapon Artillery requires preparation before shooting.
[Type] sagittam ponite Load Load projectile into weapon.
[Type] dirigite Aim Identify & aim weapon at target.
[Type] parate Prepare Prepare to fire weapon.
[Type] dimittite Fire Release projectile.
[Type] tutam reddite Secure weapons Put weapon into a safe state and await further instruction.
Miscellaneous
Parate Prepare Prepare for the next command.
Parati Ready Inform others you are prepared & ready.
Exspectate Wait for it Wait for the nunc command before executing command.
Nunc Now Execute the preceding command (Ex. "ad gladios clinate... nunc").
Sin[ister]/Gladios Right/Sword [hand] Legio XXX prefers to use gladios instead of sin.
Dex[ter]/Scuta Left/Shield [hand] Legio XXX prefers to use scuta instead of dex.
Clamate Battle cry Chant "Ro..ma! Ro..ma! Ro..ma!" in-step.
Silentium/Tacete Silence Silence any chatter in the ranks.
Accelerate Speed Up Can be prepended to other commands.
Tardate/Tarde Slow Down/Slowly Can be prepended to other commands.
